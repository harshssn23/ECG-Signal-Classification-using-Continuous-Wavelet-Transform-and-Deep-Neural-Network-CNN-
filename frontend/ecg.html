About Us

Welcome to our heart disease detection system. Our platform enables users to upload ECG signal images for analysis. Through advanced technology, images are classified into three key categories: Cardiac Arrhythmia (ARR), Congestive Heart Failure (CHF), or Normal Sinus Rhythm (NSR). The application promptly displays the classification result along with relevant messages, providing users with valuable insights. Join us in our mission to revolutionize cardiac health assessment and contribute to healthier communities worldwide.
